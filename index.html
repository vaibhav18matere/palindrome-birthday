Palindrome Birthday

Enter your birthdate and we will tell you if your birthdate is a palindrome

A palindrome is a word/number which reads the same backward as forward. This app checks your birthdate in 6 formats ddmmyyyy, mmddyyyy, yyyymmdd, ddmmyy, mmddyy, yymmdd. If your birthdate is 18 Aug. 1997, then app will check for 18081997, 08181997, 19970818, 180897, 081897, 970818
